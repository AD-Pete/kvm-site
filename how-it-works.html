02 — Export · Digital Classes

Anytime. Anywhere. Actually corrected.

A — LIBRARY

On-demand, on subscription

Full classes and broken-down tutorials, structured by level and style. Mirrored playback, speed control, weekly drops. A library, not a dump of VODs.

B — LIVE AR PARTNER

Your instructor, in the room with you

Point your camera at yourself and a live avatar of your KVM instructor appears beside you in AR — dancing the combo with you, reacting in real time, correcting in their own voice. A duet, not a scorecard.

C — WEEKLY LIVES

One floor, every dancer

Scheduled live classes with the remote crowd projected into the room. The instructor can pull you forward for a shout-out from anywhere on earth.

The studio is everywhere now.

The same AR pipeline runs in reverse: dancers at home are captured and projected into the live class — onto the studio screen, into the instructor’s view, into the stream grid. Remote students stop being an audience and become the room.

03 — Convert · KVM Training

Duolingo, for dance.

A structured path, not a content pile. Skills unlock in sequence, streaks keep you honest, and every level is passed the only way that counts — on video, verified by the same engine that dances beside you.

LV.1Groove

Find the pocket. Daily practice check-ins — film 60 seconds, keep the streak alive.

LV.2Foundation

The steps that everything else stands on. Pass by submitting video — the engine verifies your lines and timing.

LV.3Texture & combos

Leagues and cohorts — small groups progressing together, because solo grinding isn't the culture.

LV.4Freestyle

Weekly prompts, community voting. Structured training becomes actual dancing.
